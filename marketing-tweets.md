## Posting Strategy

### Timing
- Post 1-2 tweets per day
- Best times: 7-9am, 12-1pm, 7-9pm (when homeowners are active)
- Weekends: Focus on educational/story content

### Hashtag Strategy
Primary: #SolarEnergy #SolarPanels #HomeOwner
Secondary: #CleanEnergy #SolarPower #HomeImprovement #RenewableEnergy
Niche: #SolarWarranty #SolarMaintenance

### Engagement Tactics
1. Reply to anyone commenting about solar installer problems
2. Engage with solar energy news (especially bankruptcy announcements)
3. Join conversations in #Solar and #CleanEnergy communities
4. Monitor mentions of specific bankrupt solar companies

### A/B Testing
- Test different CTAs: "Get Help Now" vs "Free Consultation" vs "Learn More"
- Test emotional vs factual approaches
- Track which statistics resonate most (bankruptcy numbers vs dollar amounts)

### Additional Channels
- Consider X/Twitter Ads targeting: homeowners, solar panel owners, clean energy enthusiasts
- Location targeting: States with high solar adoption (CA, AZ, TX, FL, NV, NJ)
- Retargeting: People who visited the landing page but didn't convert
